Our Story

La Cucina was born from a simple love of food ‚Äî real, honest, delicious food that brings people together around the table.

Cooking is Our Language

Founded in 2018, La Cucina started as a small food blog written by chef Maria Rossi in her Bologna apartment. What began as a personal journal of cherished family recipes grew into one of the most beloved cooking destinations on the internet.

We believe that great cooking isn't about fancy equipment or exotic ingredients ‚Äî it's about understanding flavor, respecting tradition, and cooking with heart. Every recipe on our platform has been tested, tasted, and perfected by our team of passionate chefs.

Today, La Cucina is home to over 2,400 recipes spanning 180 world cuisines, loved by home cooks in 60+ countries. We are dedicated to keeping the art of cooking alive, joyful, and accessible to everyone.
